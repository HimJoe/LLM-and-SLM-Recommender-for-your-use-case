Understanding Token Utilization

What is a Token?

Language models don't see words like we do. They break down text into smaller units called "tokens." A token can be a word, a part of a word, or even just a punctuation mark.

A good rule of thumb is that **1 token is approximately 4 characters or 0.75 words** for common English text. The total number of tokens in your input (prompt) and the model's output (response) determines the computational cost.
